ABOUT THE PROJECT

Sleep Disorder Prediction:

This project analyzes various lifestyle and medical variables—such as age, BMI, sleep duration, physical activity, stress levels, blood pressure, and more—to predict whether an individual has a sleep disorder (Insomnia or Sleep Apnea) or not.

The model is built using a Random Forest Classifier and provides a quick way to assess sleep health based on user inputs.
